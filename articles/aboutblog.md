### **关于7rees.cc**

2020年底曾使用Hexo框架和Butterfly主题搭建过名为“琴瑟在御”的个人网站，后因个人水平有限难以将框架改造成自己心目中的模样，运营一年后因域名到期而终结。

时隔近5年，个人也从前端开发转至测试。偶然刷到了某站某篇写的相当不错的文章，又重燃了建站的念头。

这次不用框架，纯手写加上Google Gemini的辅助，甚至还有豆包的帮助，花费了两个周末以及多个工作日下班时间完成。



本站由AWS 亚马逊云EC2提供云计算服务，由阿里云DNS提供解析服务，由Cloudflare提供CDN全球内容分发服务；评论功能由Artalk提供支持，自托管于阿里云ESC云服务器，使用Docker进行部署。


**由衷感谢您的访问。**

### **About 7rees.cc**

At the end of 2020, I built a personal website named "Laihsiao" using the Hexo framework and the Butterfly theme. Later, due to my limited technical skills at the time, I found it difficult to customize the framework into the look I envisioned. After one year of operation, the site came to an end as the domain expired.

Nearly five years later, I have transitioned from front-end development to testing. I happened to come across a well-written article online, which reignited my desire to build a website.

This time, I didn't use any frameworks. It was purely hand-coded with the assistance of Google Gemini and Doubao, taking two weekends and several workdays after hours to complete.

This site is powered by AWS (Amazon Web Services) EC2 for cloud computing, Alibaba Cloud DNS for resolution services, and Cloudflare for global CDN content delivery. The comment system is supported by Artalk, self-hosted on an Alibaba Cloud ECS server, and deployed using Docker.
